To promote and preserve civil order?

the preceding functions can be summarized into two broad categories: enforcing the laws and maintaining order. Between these two broad functions, order maintenance constitutes the bulk of police officers’ daily activities (Senna and Siegel 1999) and therefore presents many opportunities for face-to-face contact with the public and complaints against the police.
