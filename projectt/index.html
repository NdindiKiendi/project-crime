MULIKA UHALIFU

Mulika Uhalifu is an online crime reporting system launched by The Kenya Police
National Police Service that provides an easily available crime reporting platform as an alternative of visisting the physical police stations to report a crime.
We seek to imrove the crime rate of our country with the aim of making a country more peaceful and with reduced crime rates by making sure that no crime committed goes unreported
and providing an efficient way of reporting the crime, being able to follow up with the report and see a close to the reported case.
Usiachwe nyuma,report a crime and help us keep our country safe!
